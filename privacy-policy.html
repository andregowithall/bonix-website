We use cookies to improve your experience on our site. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use the following types:

  • Strictly necessary cookies — required for the site to function (e.g., storing your cookie consent preference). These do not require consent.
  • Analytics cookies — used to understand how visitors interact with the site (e.g., Google Analytics). These are only set if you click "Accept" on the cookie banner.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or cookies. You can also configure your browser to refuse all cookies — please consult your browser's help documentation for instructions.
